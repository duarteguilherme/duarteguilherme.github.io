My name is Guilherme Jardim Duarte. I am a PhD candidate in the Operations, Information, and Decisions Department at the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

My primary research interests include causal inference and machine learning. I have developed general algorithms for partial identification and have also investigated issues related to transportability, partial/point identification in factorial experiments, and the automation of causal inference. In the past, I have worked in journalism and data science.
