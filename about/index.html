About

Hi. My name is Guilherme Duarte. I work as a data scientist for a news website in Brazil. I’m also a Doctor in Constitutional Law at University of Sao Paulo (Brazil). I apply models to political and general phenomena in order to understand its main features.

I’m addicted to R and also program in Python. My interests are programming, law, political science, methodology, statistics, data science, and music (seriously).
