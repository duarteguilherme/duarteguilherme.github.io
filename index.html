Hi. My name is Guilherme Duarte. I work as a data scientist and data editor for a news website in Brazil, Jota (https://jota.info), where I write data based articles on elections and Brazilian legislative and judiciary power.

I am a Doctor in Constitutional Law at University of Sao Paulo (Brazil), but I also present my research in Political Science conferences. My research interests are: causality, Bayesian inference, legislative studies, elections, and judicial politics.

Thanks for visiting!
